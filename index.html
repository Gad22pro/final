Programs We Offer

Training Pathways

⚽ Ages 6 – 10

Junior Stars

Where every champion begins. Fun, foundational training in a safe and supportive environment.

  • Ball control & basic technique
  • Teamwork & sportsmanship
  • Weekend training sessions
  • Small-sided match play
  • Free kit for enrolled players
🏃 Ages 11 – 14

Youth Elite

Serious football for serious players. Tactical training and competitive league experience.

  • Advanced tactical training
  • Physical conditioning program
  • Regional league participation
  • Video analysis sessions
  • Individual performance tracking
🎯 Ages 15 – 17

Senior Academy

Elite preparation for the next level. National competitions and professional pathway guidance.

  • Pro-grade coaching staff
  • National competition preparation
  • Strength & conditioning
  • Scouting exposure & trials
  • Career development guidance
Not sure which program is right?
Contact our coaches and we'll find the best fit.
Our Story

Built on Passion,
Defined by Excellence

Founded in 2021, Rising Sport Academy was born from a simple belief: every child deserves world-class football education. From a small training ground, we have grown into one of Rwanda's exciting football academies.

Our coaches bring professional experience, combining modern tactics with fundamentals — discipline, teamwork, resilience, and love for the game.

🏆 Excellence

We settle for nothing less than our best.

🤝 Teamwork

Great teams built on trust and respect.

💪 Discipline
